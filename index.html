A federally licensed insolvency and restructuring firm — built for the moments when clarity matters most.

Albert Gelman Inc. serves individuals, business owners, and financial institutions across Canada. We provide custom-tailored solutions for proposals, bankruptcies, restructurings, and receiverships — with senior judgment at every turn.
